This site will show you the most popular music in the disco era composed by artists such as the Bees Gees, Donna Summer, and Kool & the gang. Although it is one of the most shortest lived rage in the music industry, disco has changed our world.

Music

Disco is a genre of music that became popular in the United States in the 1970s. Disco was known for its upbeat catchy tempo played by electric pianos, synthesizers (a type of piano) and guitars. The vocals are also known to be repetitive

History

Disco is known to come from the 1970s, but did you know that it wasn’t popular until the late 70s and early 80s? Disco was slowing introduced in the early 1970s but didn’t take a boom until the movie “Saturday Night Fever” with John Travolta, was premiered. After the premiere, disco was at its peak in popularity.
